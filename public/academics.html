Academics

Kidergarten (Jr and Senior)

Kindergarten is an early education program for children aged four to six. Coined by Friedrich Fröbel in 1837, the term means "children's garden" in German. Key features include a focus on overall development through play-based learning, a foundational curriculum in basic subjects, social skill development, preparation for elementary school, and a nurturing environment facilitated by teachers. It serves as a crucial transition from home or preschool to formal schooling.
